Understanding Ports & Shipping

What Ports Do

Learn about loading, unloading, and how ports help ships arrive and depart safely. Ports serve as crucial hubs for global trade, handling over 90% of world trade by volume.

Discover container ships, tankers, cruise ships, and other vessel types. Each ship is designed for specific cargo or purposes, from transporting goods to carrying passengers.
